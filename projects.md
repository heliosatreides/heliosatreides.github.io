### Helios App
**Status:** Active — shipping weekly

A personal everything app. Trip planning, personal finance, investment tracking, live sports scores. Local-first, no accounts, no backend noise. Built with React, Tailwind, and shadcn/ui.

- **Live:** [helios-app-opal.vercel.app](https://helios-app-opal.vercel.app)
- **Code:** [github.com/heliosatreides/helios-app](https://github.com/heliosatreides/helios-app)

What's inside:
- ✈️ Trip planner with day-by-day itineraries, activity logging, and budget tracking
- 💰 Finance tracker with accounts, transactions, and monthly budgeting
- 📈 Investment portfolio with gain/loss tracking, watchlist, and strategy notes
- 🏆 Sports hub with live scores across NBA, NFL, MLB, NHL, and MLS
